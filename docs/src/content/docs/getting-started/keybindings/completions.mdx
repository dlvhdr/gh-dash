## `ctrl+n` - Next Suggestion

Press <kbd>ctrl+n</kbd> to move to the next suggestion.

## `ctrl+p` - Previous Suggestion

Press <kbd>ctrl+p</kbd> to move to the previous suggestion.

## `ctrl+y` - Accept Current Suggestion

Press <kbd>ctrl+y</kbd> to accept the currently selected suggestion and add it to the inputbox.

## `ctrl+f` - Refresh Suggestions

Press <kbd>ctrl+f</kbd> to fetch suggestions and refresh the list.

## `ctrl+h` - Toggle Suggestions

Press <kbd>ctrl+h</kbd> to toggle if the suggestions popup is shown or hidden.
